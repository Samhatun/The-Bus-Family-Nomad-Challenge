🚌 The Bus Family Nomad Challenge

🎯 Concept

A couple chooses a minimalist nomadic life, raising three children in a converted bus while traveling across every lot in the world. No settling down, no romance distractions—just survival, parenting, and constant movement.

⚙️ Setup Rules

  • 2 adult Sims (a couple)
  • 3 children (any mix: toddler/child/teen)
  • 1 pet (cat or dog)
  • Lot type: Tiny Home Residential
  • Build a bus-style house (max 32 tiles)
  • Aging off for 365 sim days
  • No new households allowed in the world

🏡 Bus Requirements

  • Sleeping space for all Sims
  • Bathroom
  • Small kitchen
  • Pet essentials
  • Must look like a bus/van conversion

🚚 Core Rules

  • Move to a new lot every Sim day (stay 12 hours minimum)
  • Visit 365 different lots (repetition allowed)
  • No revisiting a lot before 7 days
  • Each day: befriend 1 new Sim

🚫 Restrictions

  • No romance outside the couple
  • No full-time careers
  • No large builds outside bus
  • No adding new household members

🎯 Win Condition

  • Visit every lot in the world
  • Keep all children in household
  • Pet survives
  • No rule violations
